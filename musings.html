To Life...?

From a regular feature in school examinations to the occsional application to the authorities, Letters have somehow become an integral parts of our professional lives. Yes we seldom communicate with letters nowadays unless it's an official purpose beckoning. Letters have been long replaced by it's technological brother The Email. And yes before you could blink an eye Instant Messengers were there for the Tech Savy Youth. With talks of a cashless economy doing the rounds, we might as well have to say goodbye to a paper-based life style. But the spirit of ink blotting a parchment shall live on for as long as humanity exists...
